The Thesis

India builds. India ships. But serious, structured feedback is rare. Most founders rely on Reddit threads, friends, or silence. This initiative exists to provide thoughtful, written evaluation from experienced builders, privately and without theatrics.

01. Structured Written Evaluation

Each selected product receives a minimum of five detailed written reviews. Feedback focuses on clarity of problem, target audience, UX logic, technical feasibility, and market positioning. This is asynchronous. No live sessions. No presentations required.

02. Domain Perspective

1–3 experienced builders provide contextual commentary. These are written critiques based on their professional experience. There are no calls, workshops, or mentorship commitments, only thoughtful, documented feedback.

03. The Structural Report

Feedback is consolidated into a structured summary highlighting: strengths, blind spots, competitive landscape risks, and recommended next experiments.

04. Founding Recognition

All selected projects become Founding Members of ProBharat and receive permanent recognition when the public directory launches. Top 3 projects receive additional distinction.

What This Is Not

This is not an accelerator, incubator, investment program, or classroom. No equity is taken. No payment is required. Participation is fully remote and feedback-based.

The ProBharat Initiative

This validation cohort is the founding layer of ProBharat, a structured discovery and feedback network for Indian builders.

ProBharat is being built to document, validate, and surface serious products emerging from India — across sectors, states, and disciplines.
